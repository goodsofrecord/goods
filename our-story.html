  • OUR STORY

We’re Kush, Sathish, and Trey. We came together because we believed we could shift the way guys think about—and buy – their favorite products. [It’s a bold mission, but most of the good ones are.

Like most guys, we’ve bought countless products during our lives. But we always come back to a select few. It’s those quality goods, the ones made with pride and made with passion. The ones we love telling stories about. The ones we’ve never seen used by another person. The ones that only get better use. It’s a duffel made by a century old Seattle bagmaker, selvage jeans made by an obsessive denim-head in Brooklyn, or an heirloom veg-tan wallet from Minnesota.

There are plenty of wonderful products made right here in America, waiting to be discovered, and many more pioneers willing join the adventure. And that’s how Goods of Record was born: a desire to journey to the corners of the country, to find the best products, to support the best makers, and bring them to you through amazing storytelling.

Our mission in one sentence? To connect guys with their goods.
